## KingMidas Games Partners with 01.tech to Expand Global Reach
![image](https://publiccdn.kingdomhall729.com/website/images/0421_01tech.png)

**Dubai, UAE** – **21 April 2026** – KingMidas Games, a leading iGaming provider, has announced a strategic partnership with 01.tech, a premier iGaming aggregator boasting a portfolio of more than 15,000 titles.

Through this collaboration, 01.tech will integrate over 150 games from KingMidas Games into its platform, spanning a diverse range of genres, including the studio’s latest slot offerings, arcade-style experiences, and signature next-generation titles. This integration significantly enhances the accessibility of KingMidas Games’ content, enabling operators worldwide to deliver richer and more engaging gaming experiences to their players.

**Sargis Khachatryan, Commercial Director at KingMidas Games**, commented: “Our partnership with 01.tech represents a significant strategic step forward for KingMidas Games. As one of the industry’s leading aggregators with an extensive network of top-tier operator relationships across global markets, 01.tech provides an exceptional platform for us to scale our content distribution. By bringing our portfolio of next-generation titles to their ecosystem, we are not only expanding our reach but also reinforcing our commitment to empowering operators with innovative and high-performance gaming experiences.”

**Inna Polly, Head of Game Aggregator at 01.tech**, said: “Working with KingMidas Games proves that partnerships are not about quick wins, but about long-term collaboration built on trust in both the product and the team. Together with the KingMidas Games team, we have gone through key stages of product development within the 01.tech game aggregator. Even now, we see strong interest in their content from our partners, which confirms that we are moving in the right direction. We look forward to further developing and scaling this collaboration month by month. I’m confident that partnerships like this will shape the evolution of gaming content and open up new opportunities for all market participants.”

This collaboration underscores both companies’ shared vision of driving innovation and delivering value across the iGaming ecosystem, while enabling operators to stay competitive in an increasingly dynamic market.

**About 01.tech**

01.tech is a technology partner for iGaming operators, providing a unified infrastructure to launch and scale gaming businesses across global markets. The company brings together a full product ecosystem, including a White Label Platform, Game Aggregator, and Betting solution, designed to work as a single system.

At the core of 01.tech is a focus on product consistency and operational efficiency. Through a single API and unified back office, operators can manage content, payments, and player engagement without fragmented integrations.

The company works with partners who aim to build sustainable iGaming products, focusing on long-term growth, strong retention, and scalable architecture.
