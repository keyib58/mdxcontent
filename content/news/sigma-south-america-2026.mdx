## KingMidas Games to Showcase Next-Generation Portfolio at BiS SiGMA South America 2026
![image](https://publiccdn.kingdomhall729.com/website/images/0401_news.png)

**São Paulo, Brazil** – **1 April 2026** – KingMidas Games, a leading iGaming provider, has announced its participation at BiS SiGMA South America 2026. The event will take place from 6 to 9 April 2026 at the Transamerica Expo Center, where KingMidas Games will be exhibiting at **Booth P105**.

As part of its continued expansion across global markets, KingMidas Games will present a diverse portfolio of games designed to elevate player engagement and support operators in delivering differentiated experiences. Visitors can expect a comprehensive showcase spanning table and dice games, alongside an expanding suite of slot titles under its latest product innovations.

Among the highlights are standout local favourites such as Jogo de Bozó and Bicho, expertly crafted to capture the spirit, energy and playstyles of Latin American audiences. These titles go beyond localisation to deliver familiar and high-impact experiences that resonate instantly with players, driving stronger engagement and long-term retention across the South American market.

In addition, standout titles such as Chicken Crossy, a widely recognised and beloved crash game, will be featured alongside a growing portfolio of exclusive Japanese Intellectual Property (IP) collaborations, delivering distinctive and high-impact content that cannot be found elsewhere in the market.

“BiS SiGMA South America represents an important opportunity for us to deepen our presence in one of the fastest-growing iGaming regions globally,” said **Sean Auret, Global Head at KingMidas Games**. “Our focus is on delivering next-generation content and exclusive IPs that empower our partners to stand out in an increasingly competitive landscape.”

KingMidas Games invites all attendees to visit **Booth P105** to experience its latest innovations firsthand and connect with the team to explore how its solutions can add value to South America’s evolving iGaming ecosystem.
