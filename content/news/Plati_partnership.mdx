## KingMidas Games and Plati+ Unite to Expand Premium Content Distribution Across Global Markets
![image](https://publiccdn.kingdomhall729.com/website/images/0317_news.png)

**Dubai, UAE** – **17 March 2026** – KingMidas Games, a leading iGaming provider, has announced a strategic partnership with Plati+, the next-generation aggregation platform created by the team behind Platipus. The agreement will see a selection of KingMidas Games’ top-performing titles integrated into the Plati+ platform, strengthening global distribution channels and broadening access to premium, high-performance content for operators worldwide.

The collaboration reflects a shared commitment to quality, innovation and scalable growth. For KingMidas Games, the integration represents a significant step in expanding its international footprint and reinforcing its position as a leading provider of next-generation iGaming content. For Plati+, the addition of KingMidas Games enhances an already diverse aggregation ecosystem with distinctive titles designed to drive engagement and retention.

**Sargis Khachatryan, Commercial Director at KingMidas Games**, said: “Partnering with Plati+ enables us to further amplify the reach of our portfolio through a platform that prioritises strong connectivity and operational efficiency. Their aggregation capabilities and international outlook make them a valuable partner as we continue scaling our presence globally. We’re thrilled to collaborate with a team that shares our standards for quality and innovation, and we look forward to delivering our high-quality and engaging content to a wider network of operators and players.”

**Artashes Vardanyan, Sales Manager at Plati+**, commented: “We’re delighted to partner with KingMidas Games, a studio that shares our commitment to delivering innovative and highly engaging gaming experiences. By integrating their diverse and distinctive portfolio into our platform, we continue to strengthen our content offering and provide operators with an even greater variety across genres and player preferences. This collaboration not only enhances our aggregation ecosystem but also supports our strategic focus on expanding high-quality content across regulated and international markets.”

By combining KingMidas Games’ creative depth and performance-led development with Plati+’s growing aggregation network and technical expertise, the partnership delivers meaningful value to operators seeking differentiated, high-calibre content. Together, both companies reinforce their ambitions to elevate industry standards and drive sustainable growth across global markets.

**About KingMidas Games**

KingMidas Games is a leading iGaming provider delivering next-generation solutions that combine creativity, innovation, and technology. With a portfolio of over 150 high-performing titles spanning Cards, Chips, Classics, Dice, IP-based Games, Lottery, Roulette, Slots, and Virtual Experiences, KingMidas Games empowers operators to accelerate growth and maximise player engagement. Recognised with multiple iGaming accolades, including a nomination for Game Studio of the Year at the SiGMA Asia Awards 2025 and shortlisted at the Global Gaming Awards EMEA 2026, KingMidas Games continues to cement its position as a global leader in high-quality and innovative content development. All KingMidas Games content is compliant and certified by leading testing laboratories, including BMM Testlabs, Gaming Labs International, and more.
