## KingMidas Games Doubles Down on ICE Barcelona 2026
![image](https://publiccdn.kingdomhall729.com/website/images/e-invite_ice2026_1080x1920-working.png)
**Barcelona, Spain, 12 Jan 2026** – KingMidas Games, a leading iGaming provider, will exhibit at ICE Barcelona 2026, taking place from **19 to 21 January 2026** at Fira Barcelona Gran Via. Located at **Booth No. 1F50** and spanning over 115 square metres, the KingMidas Games stand will spotlight its latest game releases alongside its growing slate of Intellectual Property (IP) collaborations.

ICE Barcelona remains one of the iGaming industry’s most influential global meeting points, and KingMidas Games will arrive with an ambitious programme designed to strengthen engagement with operators, aggregators, and industry decision-makers. A packed line-up of marketing initiatives and on-site activations will create multiple opportunities for meaningful conversations, hands-on gameplay, and deeper discovery of KingMidas Games’ expanding portfolio.

KingMidas Games will also serve as the Official Hydration Sponsor for ICE Barcelona 2026. Attendees will find KingMidas Games featured across hydration stations in all four halls, delivering prominent visibility and consistent touchpoints throughout the venue.

**Showcasing High-Demand Games and New IP-Led Experiences**

At Booth 1F50, KingMidas Games will present standout releases spanning multiple categories, including in-demand titles that continue to gain traction across global markets.

Key highlights include **Chicken Crossy**, one of KingMidas Games’ most requested games, and the highly popular **Coin Pusher** range, featuring new adaptations, including a Jurassic-themed edition debuting at ICE Barcelona 2026.

KingMidas Games will also feature its latest Slots, including **Cleopatra’s Treasure**, alongside its growing library of IP-based titles. Visitors will experience **TORIDAMA** and explore additional collaborations making their debut at ICE Barcelona 2026, reinforcing KingMidas Games’ position as a gateway to distinctive and culturally resonant content.

**Visit Booth 1F50 to Play, Connect, and Win Prizes**

KingMidas Games invites all ICE Barcelona attendees to visit Booth 1F50 to meet the team, experience the newest games first-hand, and take part in interactive demonstrations. Visitors can also enjoy on-site gameplay and walk away with attractive prizes, adding another reason to stop by, connect, and explore what’s next from KingMidas Games.
